## Reconstruction and modifications

This repository is an independent, unofficial source reconstruction and maintenance edition of release 3.0.5 for Minecraft 1.20.1 Forge. The source was reconstructed from the distributed JVM bytecode and resources, remapped to official Mojang names, made buildable with ForgeGradle, and modified to correct defects and incomplete behavior.

Because the published JAR does not contain the author's original source text, original comments, formatting and some local variable names cannot be recovered verbatim. The repository does not claim to be the author's original development workspace.
